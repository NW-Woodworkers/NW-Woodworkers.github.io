Membership

Membership is open to anyone that is interested in woodworking no matter your age or skill level. The benefits of membership are greatest for those that actively participate in all of the activities of the Northwest Woodworkers Association.

Annual dues are $20. In order to become a member, complete the membership application below and click Submit. Somebody will contact you to figure out how best to pay the dues. For questions, please contact the Membership Chair at membership@nwwoodworkers.org.
