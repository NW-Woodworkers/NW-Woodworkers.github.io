Membership

Membership is open to anyone that is interested in woodworking no matter your age or skill level. The benefits of membership are greatest for those that actively participate in all of the activities of the Northwest Woodworkers Association.

Annual dues are $20. In order to become a member, complete the membership application below and click Submit. Dues may be paid on the next page with a credit card, or by check at your first meeting. For questions, please contact the Membership Chair.
